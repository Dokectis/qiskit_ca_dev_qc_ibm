# Qiskit Quantum Computing Development Labs ⚛️ | IBM Quantum

This repository serves as my personal development sandbox and portfolio for exploring, designing, and implementing quantum algorithms using **Qiskit** and **IBM Quantum** hardware/simulators.

As a **Qiskit Advocate** and **IBM Quantum Enthusiast**, my goal is to bridge the gap between classical data engineering and the emerging field of quantum data processing.

---

### 🎯 Objective
To document my progress in mastering quantum information science, developing a deep understanding of quantum circuits, algorithms, and practical applications within the IBM Quantum ecosystem.

### 📚 Tech Stack & Tools
* **Quantum SDK:** Qiskit (Python) v1.x+ 2.3
* **Quantum Hardware/Simulators:** IBM Quantum (Aer simulator, real quantum backends via IBM Quantum Platform)
* **Environment:** Jupyter Notebooks, Python 3.x

---

### 🚀 Roadmap & Project Structure

This repository is organized by complexity and topic, acting as a progressive learning path.

#### 📁 `01_fundamentals/`
> Minimalist notebooks exploring core quantum concepts.
* **Lab 1.1:** [Hello Quantum World (Bell State)](01_fundamentals/bell_state_basic.ipynb) 🇪🇸/🇺🇸 - Creating and simulating the $|\Phi^+\rangle$ state (Entanglement).
* **Lab 1.2:** [Entanglement Exploration](01_fundamentals/entanglement_exploration.ipynb) 🇪🇸/🇺🇸 - Exploring variations of entangled states ($|\Psi^+\rangle$).
* **Dev Sandbox:** [Qiskit Run Development](01_fundamentals/qiskit_run_dev.ipynb) - General purpose testing area for circuits and simulators.
